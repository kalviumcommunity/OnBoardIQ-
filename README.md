## 1. Problem Statement

A growing startup stores employee onboarding progress, internal tool usage, and support requests in different systems. Because the information is scattered, managers and leadership cannot easily identify what is slowing down new employees during their first month.


## 2. Business Understanding

**Who faces this problem?**

- HR Team
- Engineering Managers
- Team Leads
- Operations Team
- Leadership
- Business Value
- Saves time by keeping onboarding data in one place.
- Makes it easier to track employee progress.
- Helps identify common onboarding issues.
- Supports better business decisions using data.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Centralize employee onboarding information.
- Track onboarding progress.
- Monitor internal tool usage.
- Track support requests raised by new employees.
- Help leadership identify productivity bottlenecks.

---

## 4. Stakeholders

Primary Users - HR Team, Engineering Managers, Team Leads 
Secondary Users - Operations Team, IT Support Team 
Decision Makers - Leadership, HR Head 
Business Beneficiaries - Company, HR Team, New Employees 

---

## 5. Business Research 
 
BambooHR - Provides employee onboarding, task tracking, and employee management. 
Workday - Offers HR analytics, onboarding dashboards, and workforce insights. 
Our Learning - Bringing onboarding data into one dashboard helps managers quickly identify issues and improve the onboarding process. 

---

## 6. Assumptions

- HR maintains employee records.
- Employees use internal company tools.
- Support requests are recorded in the system.
- Managers regularly monitor onboarding progress.

---

## 7. Functional Requirements

**Employee Management**
- Add employee details
- View employee profile
- Assign department and manager

**Onboarding**
- View onboarding checklist
- Track completed and pending tasks
- Show onboarding completion percentage

**Tool Usage**
- Record login activity
- Track tool usage
- Identify inactive users

**Support Tickets**
- Create support requests
- Update ticket status
- Categorize issues

**Dashboard**
- Overall onboarding progress
- Tool adoption statistics
- Common support issues
- Department-wise comparison
- Productivity trends

---

## 8. Success Metrics
- Reduce onboarding completion time by 20%.
- Achieve 90% onboarding completion within the first 30 days.
- Reduce repeated support tickets by 30%.
- Increase internal tool adoption by 25%.
- Reduce average support ticket resolution time by 15%.
